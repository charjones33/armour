Armour Magazine

The Premier Fashion Publication of Washington University in St. Louis.

As design majors, Felicia Podberesky, Jacob Lenard, and Chantal Strasburger sat discussing the lack of fashion as a frontier here at Wash U. They desired to integrate their fashionable hobbies into the WashU community. In an effort to celebrate the creative community thriving in Sam Fox (and beyond!) they founded Armour, a fashion magazine to cultivate and enrich the fashionable happenings occurring on Danforth Campus. From those humble beginnings Armour has grown into a force to be reckoned with. Drawing talent from all grades and majors, Armour helps students express and finetune their creative skills. Spanning the gamut from photography, layout design, illustration, writing and editing, the skillset of Armour's staff increases every issue.
